## Elemental Words

Elemental words provide **AP** (Attack Power) based on tier and apply elemental damage. Words change form based on slot placement:
- **Noun Slot**: Uses noun form (e.g., "Fire", "Phoenix")
- **Adjective Slots**: Uses adjective form (e.g., "Fiery", "Infernal")

## Element Families

Elements are grouped into two families, which matters for certain talents:

**World & Sky**: Fire, Water, Lightning, Earth
**Body & Soul**: Physical, Poison, Light, Dark

---

## Tier System

- **T1**: Grants 1 AP (Attack Power)
- **T2**: Grants 2 AP
- **T3**: Grants 3 AP

Higher tier words are found in shops as you progress through chapters.

PHASE EXECUTION:
  • Converter Phase: Midas Touch, Golden Reread, Liquidate
  • Threshold Phase: Fortune's Favor
  • All respect loop iteration tracking (loopIteration, triggeredThresholds)

SCALING:

  • Fortune's Favor is threshold, no scaling
  • Base values: Midas 5g, Golden Reread 10g, Liquidate 20g
